About Fleckmesh

Fleckmesh was coined from two English words – fleck (spot, dot or to mark) and mesh (a network of). These simplify Fleckmesh as “a network of dots”. It was born out of the ideology that everything in the world or in reality can be represented or depicted as “a dot” in a virtual space that maybe a group of items with similar attributes or functions. These groups maybe related by unique individual functions performed to satisfy a general need or by competitions and other factors peculiar to them.

Mission and Vision

Vision: To create an application that simplifies and redefines means of reaching and satisfying basic human (material) needs with the best possible available resources, all in one box.

Mission: To provide need seekers and service providers with one application that bundles all providers(businesses) in one box and seekers(audience) in another, then places both side by side for easy access to resources and a common means of settling service requirement at the a low cost.

The Author:

The Fleckmesh Project has been designed and developed by David Inyang-Etoh, a software developer from Ukana Ikot Etan, near Ikot Ekpene, Nigeria.

He had his first computer education in 2002, started designing Websites in 2004 and by mid 2006 he had developed his first enterprise web application with PHP/MySQL

He has since embraced numerous web technologies to enhance his programming skills, delving into enterprise web application development, webservices and mobile application development for multiple smartphone platforms

The Fleckmesh project an implementation of ten years of hard work and study in software developing on web and mobile systems. Its a demonstration of multiple technology integration which includes:


  • PHP/MySQL
  • Yii Framework
  • Twitter Bootstrap
  • Backbone Js
  • Marionette Js
  • Require Js
  • Apache Cordova
