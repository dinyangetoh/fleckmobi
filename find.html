Fleck Mobile

Fleckmesh was coined from two English words – fleck (spot, dot or to mark) and mesh (a network of). These simplify Fleckmesh as “a network of dots”. It was born out of the ideology that everything in the world or in reality can be represented or depicted as “a dot” in a virtual space that maybe a group of items with similar attributes or functions. These groups maybe related by unique individual functions performed to satisfy a general need or by competitions and other factors peculiar to them.
